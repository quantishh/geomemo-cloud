How It Works

GeoMemo's aggregation is made possible by a "Hybrid Intelligence" engine—a synergy of people and software forming a rigorous editorial pyramid.

At the foundation is our state-of-the-art monitoring technology, which scans the globe 24/7, translating local languages and identifying narrative clusters in real-time. Above that, our discovery tools filter for high-impact events. At the top, human editors make the final calls on what we feature, writing the descriptive summaries that place every story in its proper geopolitical context.
